01 · The Scope

What we're building

A complete digital rebuild — replacing the current template site with a custom-designed presence that reflects the brand and turns visitors into paying members.

I
Custom website — rebuilt from scratch in your brand colors and identity. Fast, modern, built to convert.
II
Membership system — customers sign up for VIP or Premium online and get charged automatically every month. No more chasing payments or paper sign-ups.
III
Booking handoff — a clean "Book Now" experience that sends customers straight to your existing Booksy page. No disruption to how you currently run appointments.
IV
Live integrations — Instagram feed pulled in automatically, Google reviews displayed on the site, all updating without you having to touch anything.
02 · Boundaries

What's not included

To keep the project on time and on budget, the following are explicitly out of scope. Any of these can be added as separate work later.

  • Real-time Booksy availability sync (Booksy doesn't allow this — booking redirects to your Booksy page)
  • New logo or brand identity design (using your existing logo)
  • Professional photography (using your existing photos or stock)
  • Copywriting beyond editing what you provide
  • Email marketing platform setup (Mailchimp, etc.)
  • Paid ad campaigns or SEO content production
